# Type

* [type:electric](type_electric.md) - Electric type | Strong against Water and Flying | Only weak to Ground attacks
* [type:fire](type_fire.md) - Fire type | Strong against Grass Ice Bug and Steel | Weak to Water Ground and Rock attacks
* [type:flying](type_flying.md) - Flying type | Strong against Grass Fighting and Bug | Weak to Electric Ice and Rock attacks
* [type:grass](type_grass.md) - Grass type | Strong against Water Ground and Rock | Weak to Fire Ice Poison Flying and Bug attacks
* [type:normal](type_normal.md) - Normal type | No type advantages | Weak to Fighting attacks | Immune to Ghost
* [type:poison](type_poison.md) - Poison type | Strong against Grass and Fairy | Weak to Ground and Psychic attacks
* [type:psychic](type_psychic.md) - Psychic type | Strong against Fighting and Poison | Weak to Bug Ghost and Dark attacks
* [type:water](type_water.md) - Water type | Strong against Fire Ground and Rock | Weak to Grass and Electric attacks
