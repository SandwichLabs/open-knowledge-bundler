# Trainer

* [trainer:ash](trainer_ash.md) - Ash Ketchum | Pokemon trainer from Pallet Town in Kanto | The iconic trainer who travels with Pikachu and dreams of becoming a Pokemon Master
* [trainer:brock](trainer_brock.md) - Brock | Rock type gym leader of Pewter City in Kanto | A Pokemon breeder who dreams of becoming the world's best and travels with Ash
* [trainer:gary](trainer_gary.md) - Gary Oak | Pokemon researcher and trainer from Pallet Town | Ash's rival and grandson of Professor Oak who later becomes a Pokemon researcher
* [trainer:giovanni](trainer_giovanni.md) - Giovanni | Ground type gym leader and leader of Team Rocket | A powerful and mysterious trainer who controls Mewtwo and runs a criminal organization
* [trainer:misty](trainer_misty.md) - Misty | Water type gym leader of Cerulean City in Kanto | Specializes in Water type Pokemon and is one of Ash's closest traveling companions
