# Pokemon

* [Bulbasaur](pokemon_001.md) - Bulbasaur | Grass and Poison type starter Pokemon from Kanto | A seed Pokemon with a plant bulb on its back that grows as it evolves
* [Ivysaur](pokemon_002.md) - Ivysaur | Grass and Poison type Pokemon from Kanto | The bulb on its back grows larger as it absorbs nutrients to prepare for evolution
* [Venusaur](pokemon_003.md) - Venusaur | Grass and Poison type fully evolved Pokemon from Kanto | A massive Pokemon with a giant flower on its back that absorbs sunlight
* [Charmander](pokemon_004.md) - Charmander | Fire type starter Pokemon from Kanto | A lizard Pokemon with a flame on its tail that indicates its health
* [Charmeleon](pokemon_005.md) - Charmeleon | Fire type Pokemon from Kanto | An aggressive Pokemon that wields sharp claws and a fiery tail
* [Charizard](pokemon_006.md) - Charizard | Fire and Flying type fully evolved Pokemon from Kanto | A dragon-like Pokemon that breathes intense flames hot enough to melt boulders
* [Squirtle](pokemon_007.md) - Squirtle | Water type starter Pokemon from Kanto | A tiny turtle Pokemon that sprays water from its mouth with great accuracy
* [Wartortle](pokemon_008.md) - Wartortle | Water type Pokemon from Kanto | Its fluffy tail is a symbol of longevity and is popular among older trainers
* [Blastoise](pokemon_009.md) - Blastoise | Water type fully evolved Pokemon from Kanto | A powerful Pokemon with twin water cannons on its shell that can blast through steel
* [Pikachu](pokemon_025.md) - Pikachu | Electric type Pokemon from Kanto | The iconic mouse Pokemon that stores electricity in its cheek pouches and releases powerful thunderbolts
* [Raichu](pokemon_026.md) - Raichu | Electric type evolved Pokemon from Kanto | When electricity builds up in its body it becomes aggressive and glows in the dark
* [Eevee](pokemon_133.md) - Eevee | Normal type Pokemon from Kanto | A unique Pokemon with unstable genetics that allow it to evolve into many different forms
* [Vaporeon](pokemon_134.md) - Vaporeon | Water type Eevee evolution from Kanto | Its cell composition is similar to water molecules allowing it to melt into water
* [Jolteon](pokemon_135.md) - Jolteon | Electric type Eevee evolution from Kanto | Every hair on its body is a sharp needle that launches electric attacks
* [Flareon](pokemon_136.md) - Flareon | Fire type Eevee evolution from Kanto | It stores thermal energy in its body and can heat the air to 3000 degrees
* [Mewtwo](pokemon_150.md) - Mewtwo | Psychic type legendary Pokemon from Kanto | A genetically engineered Pokemon created from Mew DNA with immense psychic power
* [Mew](pokemon_151.md) - Mew | Psychic type mythical Pokemon from Kanto | Said to contain the DNA of every Pokemon making it capable of learning any move
* [Chikorita](pokemon_152.md) - Chikorita | Grass type starter Pokemon from Johto | A gentle leaf Pokemon that loves sunbathing and uses the sweet aroma from its leaf to calm others
* [Cyndaquil](pokemon_155.md) - Cyndaquil | Fire type starter Pokemon from Johto | A timid fire mouse Pokemon with flames on its back that flare up when startled
* [Totodile](pokemon_158.md) - Totodile | Water type starter Pokemon from Johto | A small but powerful crocodile Pokemon with strong jaws that chomps on anything
